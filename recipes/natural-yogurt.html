Natural Yogurt

Description

This natural yogurt is made from just two simple ingredients: milk and yogurt culture. It's creamy, nutritious, and perfect as a base for smoothies, desserts, or enjoyed on its own.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons of plain yogurt with live cultures

Steps

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan until it reaches 180°F (82°C), then let it cool to 110°F (43°C).
  2. Stir the plain yogurt into the cooled milk until well combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into a clean jar or container with a lid.
  4. Keep the container in a warm place (110°F/43°C) for 6-8 hours or until the yogurt sets.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving to chill and thicken.
  6. Enjoy your homemade natural yogurt plain or with your favorite toppings.
