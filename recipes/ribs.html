BBQ Ribs

Description

These BBQ ribs are slow-cooked to perfection, ensuring tender, fall-off-the-bone meat. Coated in a smoky, tangy barbecue glaze, this recipe delivers a mouthwatering dish perfect for gatherings or a hearty family meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 275°F (135°C).
  2. Mix salt, pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and brown sugar in a bowl.
  3. Rub the seasoning mixture generously over both sides of the ribs.
  4. Wrap the ribs tightly in aluminum foil and place them on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the meat is tender.
  6. Remove the ribs from the oven and unwrap the foil.
  7. Brush the ribs with BBQ sauce and broil on high for 5-10 minutes to caramelize.
  8. Let the ribs rest for 10 minutes, then slice and serve.
